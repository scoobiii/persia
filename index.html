H.R. 8842 · Iran War Powers and Fiscal Responsibility Act

AN ACT

To prohibit further unauthorized military engagement against the Islamic Republic of Iran, to terminate the 2026 supplemental war appropriation, and to establish a framework for diplomatic resolution including reparations.

SEC. 4. REPARATIONS FRAMEWORK. Compensation to Iran for verified civilian infrastructure damages, not to exceed $500 billion, payable in tranches over 10 years.
